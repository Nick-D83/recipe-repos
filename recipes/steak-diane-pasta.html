Steak Diane Pasta

This easy and delicious pasta dish will make you the favourite parent for any child and best suited to get back at your significant other.

Ingredients

  • 375g dried farfalle (bows-tie) pasta
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 20g butter
  • 300g beef rump medallions, excess fat trimmed
  • 300g Swiss brown mushrooms, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) brandy
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 300ml carton thickened cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, plus extra chopped leaves, to serve
  • 80g (2/3 cup) coarsely grated mozzarella
  • Mixed salad leaves, to serve

Cooking Method

  1. Cook pasta in a large saucepan of salted boiling water following packet directions or until al dente. Drain, reserving 125ml (1/2 cup) cooking liquid.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil and butter in a large, deep oven-proof frying pan over high heat. Season beef. Cook beef for 2 minutes each side or until browned. Transfer to a plate and set aside for 5 minutes to rest. Thinly slice (reserve resting juices).
  3. Heat pan over high heat. Add mushroom and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes or until lightly browned. Add garlic and cook, stirring, for 30 seconds or until fragrant. Add brandy and cook for 1 minute or until reduced by half. Add Worcestershire sauce and mustard and stir until combined. Add cream, reserved resting juices and cooking liquid and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 minutes or until sauce thickens slightly. Add the beef, pasta and parsley. Stir to combine. Season.
  4. Preheat grill on high. Top pasta mixture with mozzarella. Grill for 2 minutes or until light golden. Sprinkle with extra parsley. Serve with salad leaves. Watch our step-by-step video below to see how to make our Cheeseburger and chip pie.
